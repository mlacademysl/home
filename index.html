About Us

Machine Learning Academy Sri Lanka introduces students to the concepts and terminology of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML). By the end of these trainings, students will be able to select and apply ML services to resolve business problems. They will also be able to label, build, train and deploy a custom ML model. These trainings contains content delivered through lectures, hands on labs and project work.
